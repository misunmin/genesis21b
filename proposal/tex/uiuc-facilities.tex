
The University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign National Center
for Supercomputing Applications (NCSA) hosts {\bf Delta},
which includes 64 NVIDIA H200 GPUs, 440 NVIDIA A100 GPUs,
and 124 CPU nodes, each equipped with 128 AMD Milan cores.
In addition, Co-PIs Fischer and Olson have significant local
computing resources in the Scientific Computing Laboratory,
including a GPU-based workstation with two NVIDIA V100 GPUs
and a CPU-based workstation with 0.5 TB of RAM.
