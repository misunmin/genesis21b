
UIUC's National Center for Supercomputing Applications currently
hosts {\bf Delta},  which comprises 64 NVIDIA H200s and 440 A100s
as well as 124 CPU nodes, each with 128 AMD Milan cores.
In addition, co-I's Fischer and Olson have significant resources
in the Scientific Computing Lab, including a GPU-based workstation
with two NVIDIA V100s and a CPU-based workstation with 1/2 TB of RAM.
