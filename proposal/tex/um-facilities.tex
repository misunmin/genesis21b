The University of Michigan Advanced Research Computing (ARC) Center
provides large-scale HPC resources, including the {\bf Great Lakes} cluster,
a campus-wide GPU-enabled system with~13,000 CPU cores and
NVIDIA GPU nodes interconnected via high-speed InfiniBand.
Additional secure and specialized computing resources are available
through the {\bf Armis2} and {\bf Lighthouse} systems. Co-PI Vinuesa has also
acquired a dedicated computational cluster consisting of
2,048 CPU cores and 24 NVIDIA H200 GPUs.
